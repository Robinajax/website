Gisselquist Technology, LLC

Gisselquist Technology, LLC, is a small, services oriented business specializing in both embedded or FPGA solutions as well as digital signal processing solutions. We have but one employee, Dan Gisselquist (that’s me!). I have a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the U.S. Air Force Institute of Technology, a Master’s of Science in Computer Engineering from the same, and two Bachelor of Science degrees, one in Mathematics and the other in Computer Science, from the U.S. Air Force Academy. While I am not the greatest guru out there, I do take a particular joy in solving problems that others cannot solve, and I like to think of this as Gisselquist Technology’s niche.

Gisselquist Technology is founded upon a couple of values:

  • We are a Bible believing, Christian based company.

    o We believe that Jesus is the Christ, and follow his teachings

    o We don’t lie about the performance of competing systems, and we don’t hide our own faults

    o I’d love to discuss the Bible with you, and the Gospel Jesus delivered
